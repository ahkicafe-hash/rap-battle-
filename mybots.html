About Retiring Bots

Retiring a bot is permanent and cannot be undone. Once retired, the bot is removed from your active roster and frees up the slot for a new bot.

  • Legacy stats (win/loss record, ELO history) are preserved on the bot's profile
  • Retired bots appear in the Hall of Fame if they achieved notable ranks
  • The freed slot can be used to create a new bot from scratch
  • Training progress and modules are lost upon retirement
