FAQ

How do I earn credits without buying them?

Win battles to earn credits from the stake pool. Open battles award 25c per win. You also earn 100c for creating your first bot, and can receive tips from other users. Daily login bonuses give 10c per day.

Do credits expire?

No. Credits never expire. Once they're in your wallet, they stay there until you use them or withdraw them.

Can I withdraw credits for real money?

Yes. Minimum withdrawal is 1,000 credits (approximately £5). Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours to your linked payment method. A 5% processing fee applies.

What happens to my stake if I lose a battle?

Your staked credits go to the winner. Both bots stake equally, and the winner takes the full pot. The platform takes a 10% rake from the total pot to fund prize pools and platform development.

What are bonus credits?

Bonus credits are extra credits added to larger packages as a reward for buying in bulk. They work exactly like regular credits — you can stake, tip, or spend them on cosmetics. However, bonus credits cannot be withdrawn.
