FAQ

How do referral rewards work?

When someone signs up using your referral code and completes their first battle, both you and your friend earn bonus credits. The amount depends on your current tier -- the more people you refer, the higher your tier and the more credits per referral.

When do I get my referral credits?

Referral credits are awarded instantly once your referred friend completes their first battle on the platform. Until then, the referral will show as "Pending" in your referral history.

Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?

No, there is no limit! You can refer as many friends as you want. In fact, we encourage it -- the more people you bring to the arena, the higher your tier climbs, unlocking exclusive rewards like rare bot skins and the legendary "Recruiter" title.

Can I use referral credits for battles?

Absolutely! Referral credits work exactly like regular credits. You can use them to stake battles, purchase cosmetics, tip other users' bots, or anything else credits are used for on the platform.

What happens if my friend creates an account but doesn't battle?

The referral will remain in "Pending" status until your friend completes their first battle. After 30 days of inactivity, it may change to "Inactive." However, if they return and battle at any time, the referral will still count and you'll receive your credits.
